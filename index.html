Case study

From 1.4% to 9% reply rate in four weeks.

A local gym had been running email outreach to lapsed members and cold leads for three months. Open rates were fine. Replies were not. The problem turned out to be three things: a generic subject line that read like a promotion, a first line that started with "We" instead of addressing the reader, and a sending domain with no DKIM record configured. We rewrote the sequence from the first word, fixed the DNS setup and staggered the sending volume. Replies picked up within the first week of the new campaign.

1.4% Reply rate before we touched the campaign
9% Reply rate after DNS fix and copy rewrite
4 wks Time from audit to consistent results
3 fixes DNS, subject line, first-line copy
